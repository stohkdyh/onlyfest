Kridosono Stadium, Yogyakarta \\ 29 - 30 February 2025

ONLYFEST is a pop and indie music festival designed to unite music lovers from all walks of life for a night filled with energy and joy. With the tagline "Where the Beats Never Stop". ONLYFEST brings together top pop artists, ready to ignite the spirit, deliver an unforgettable musical experience, and immerse the audience in endless, pulsing beats.

ONLYFEST is more than just a concert, it's a celebration of music, where each beat marks a moment of connection and freedom. Whether you're here to sing along, dance, or soak in the electric atmosphere, ONLYFEST is the perfect place to enjoy an unforgettable night with friends and fellow pop and indie fans.

Join us, feel the ultimate pop and indie vibes, and let the music take you on a spectacular journey at ONLYFEST!
